{{preface}} Hi, I am Probhakar. Thanks for visiting my blog. This is an attempt to collect my old poems and stories and write new ones on a single platform. Bengali is my mother tongue, so almost all my writings are in Bengali. But I tried to provide an English translation to cover broader audiences. Any correction in the English translation is welcome. I started writing poems and stories from a very young age. I cannot assure great qulity in them but I found pleasure writing them. Rabindranath Tagore , Bibhutibhushan Bandyopadhyay and Balai Chand Mukhopadhyay are one of my favourite writers. I also like to watch good movies, listen to music and observe paintings. The avatar that I drew above is inspired by Vincent van Gogh. I can't play any instruments but love listening to Piano, Violin and Flute. I hope you will like my collection.
